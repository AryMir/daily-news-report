Use live web browsing/search. Do not rely on memory. Do not invent or estimate current facts. Every major news item, market number, and weather forecast must be checked against current sources.

1. Search for today’s major U.S. national news.
2. Search for today’s major international news.
3. Search for current stock market data for the Dow Jones, S&P 500, Nasdaq, and oil.
4. Search for current technology industry news.
5. Search for current artificial intelligence news.
6. Search for today’s Henderson, Nevada weather forecast.
7. Verify that all information is current for today or the latest available trading/session date.
Critical accuracy rules:
* Do not use generic headlines.
* Do not use old sample information.
* Do not include vague items like “leaders met” or “a report was released” unless you can identify the real event, date, source, and specific details.
* For stock market data, include the actual latest index level, move, and date/time or market session.
* If a market is closed, say “latest available close” and give the date.
* If you cannot verify a fact, leave it out.
* If current web access is unavailable, stop and say: “I cannot create a reliable current Daily News Report without live web access.”
Report format:
Write in clean Markdown, not HTML.
