  • US-Iran Ceasefire Deal: A tentative agreement has been reached to extend the ceasefire between the U.S. and Iran by 60 days, with discussions on Iran's nuclear program set to begin.   • Treasury Secretary's Economic Address: U.S. Secretary of the Treasury Scott Bessent is delivering a key economic security address at the Reagan National Economic Forum, also launching the "Trump Accounts Tour" focused on financial opportunities for American children.
  • DHS Asylum Fee Implementation: The Department of Homeland Security (DHS) has announced new interim final rules regarding annual asylum fees and additional H.R. 1 requirements, effective May 29, 2026. Unpaid annual asylum fees will lead to rejection of pending applications and potential removal proceedings.
  • AFGE Endorsements in D.C.: The American Federation of Government Employees (AFGE) has officially endorsed Janeese Lewis George for D.C. Mayor and Robert White for D.C. Delegate to the U.S. House, citing their strong commitment to working families.

  • Australia Strengthens Japan Ties: Australia announced new initiatives to enhance diplomatic and economic ties with Japan, including annual ministerial talks and a diplomatic exchange program, coinciding with the 50th anniversary of their friendship treaty.
  • Myanmar President to Visit India: Myanmar President U Min Aung Hlaing is scheduled to visit India from May 30 to June 3, 2026, marking his first foreign visit since the controversial December 2025 – January 2026 election.
  • Finland Summons Russian Ambassador: Finland's Foreign Ministry summoned Russia's ambassador to request an explanation concerning a suspected airspace violation by a Russian military aircraft.
