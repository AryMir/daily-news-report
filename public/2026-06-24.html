U.S. National News

1. Senate votes to halt Iran war operations. The U.S. Senate voted 50–48 on June 23 to direct President Trump to halt military operations against Iran, matching earlier House action and marking a major congressional challenge under the War Powers Act. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/us/us-senate-joins-house-voting-halt-iran-war-rebuking-trump-2026-06-23/)

2. Trump links housing bill signing to voter ID push. President Trump canceled a planned signing of a bipartisan affordable housing bill and went to Capitol Hill to pressure Senate Republicans to support the SAVE America Act, which would require photo ID for federal voting and proof of citizenship for voter registration. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/us/senate-visit-trump-push-voter-id-bill-that-republicans-say-cant-pass-2026-06-24/) | [AP](https://apnews.com/article/968c1454ede461d2db413790670c07df)

3. Millions lose SNAP benefits after federal changes. More than 4.7 million Americans have lost Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program benefits since changes tied to President Trump’s 2025 tax and spending law, with Arizona showing the steepest decline. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/us/millions-lose-food-stamps-under-trump-cuts-arizona-is-hardest-hit-2026-06-24/)

4. Supreme Court nears major rulings on presidential power. The Supreme Court is approaching the end of its term with pending cases involving birthright citizenship, removal of Federal Reserve Governor Lisa Cook, independent agency firings, and immigrant legal protections. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/legal/government/trumps-power-takes-center-stage-us-supreme-courts-home-stretch-2026-06-24/)

5. New York primaries strengthen Mayor Zohran Mamdani’s influence. Three congressional candidates backed by New York City Mayor Zohran Mamdani won Democratic primaries, increasing the influence of the democratic socialist wing in New York politics. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/us/mamdanis-power-play-kennedys-tiktoks-four-things-watch-tuesdays-primaries-2026-06-23/)
